EAS Design

In this particular project the point was to design an Emergency Alert System that was easy to navigate and hard to send accidental alerts. My design was focused around a computer dashboard with simple tabs for everything. Each type of action had a confirmation process as well that had different lengths depending on the influences the alerts could have.
I learned how to design with my user in mind and not just myself. Although the design is fairly attentaive to style and color, it is simple and easy to navigate.
